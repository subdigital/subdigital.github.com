February ALT.NET Geek Dinner

Feb 3rd, 2009

Claudio already announced the next ALT.NET Geek Dinner.  It will be held at Star Pizza again.  Last time at Freebird’s there was plenty of good burritos, but there was also loud background music, which made conversations hard to hear.

Star Pizza has a quiet-ish upstairs that is much better suited to our group.  The topic this month will be “Organizing the Conference” (http://houston.altnetconf.com).  Bring ideas & an appetite!  See you there.
